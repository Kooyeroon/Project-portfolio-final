About

This web application serves as a platform for analyzing load effects, including shear force and bending moment, within a beam. Furthermore, it generates shear force and bending moment diagrams, crucial for understanding load magnitudes along the beam's length. This functionality facilitates straightforward analysis for students, professionals, and researchers alike.
